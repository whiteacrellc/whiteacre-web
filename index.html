About Us

WhiteAcre Software is a consulting firm specializing in creating custom applications for various platforms. Founded by Tom Whittaker, we bring innovative solutions to your software needs.

WhiteAcre Software is owned by WhiteAcre Properties LLC, a Limited Liability Company incorporated in Madison, Wisconsin. WhiteAcre Properties LLC specializes in Mobile Application Development, and its premier product is KyberChat, an end-to-end encrypted communication application designed to provide protection from the upcoming Quantum Computing environment.

Tom Whittaker, based in Wanda, Minnesota, is the owner and lead developer. With a passion for technology, Tom is easily distracted by shiny objects, which fuels his curiosity and creativity in software development.
